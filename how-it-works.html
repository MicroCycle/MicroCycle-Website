How It Works

Transform your 3D print waste into beautiful, laser-cuttable sheets with our simple three-step process.

The MicroCycler

A Compact, Powerful Heat Press

The MicroCycler is designed specifically for maker spaces, schools, and individual creators. Our compact heat press fits on any workbench and transforms PLA waste into stunning, colorful sheets that can be laser cut into new creations.

  • Compact desktop design
  • Easy to operate
  • Low-cost solution for makers
  • Creates laser-cuttable sheets
MicroCycler Machine Image
The Process

Three Simple Steps

From waste to beautiful materials in minutes

Grinding Process

1 Grind

Collect your failed prints, support structures, and other PLA waste. Use a standard grinder or shredder to break them down into small pieces. The smaller the pieces, the better the final result!

Pressing Process

2 Press

Spread your PLA shreds evenly in the MicroCycler, layered with clear acrylic sheets. The heat press melts and fuses the materials together, creating a solid, beautiful sheet with unique patterns.

Finished Creations

3 Use

Your new custom sheets are ready! Take them to a laser cutter to create jewelry, ornaments, signs, home decor, and more. Each sheet is completely unique based on the colors you used.
