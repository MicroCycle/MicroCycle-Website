3D Printing Has a Waste Problem

3D printing is becoming more accessible and popularized, but this incredible technology has its downsides. Failed prints, support structures, and test pieces all add up.

One third of 3D print filament is wasted
100,000s of kg of PLA waste yearly
Millions of $ wasted each year

This is where MicroCycle comes in

Our innovative heat press transforms your 3D printing waste into something beautiful and useful.

Low-cost solution for makers
Small footprint for any workshop
Beautiful, laser-cuttable sheets

1

2

Press

3

Use
